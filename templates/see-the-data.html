Our Data

This project was developed using open sourced datasets from Airbnb that detail the properties available for rent on their platform in different cities. Our models offer rental owners a data-driven estimate on how they should value their property in Amsterdam.

After a thorough analysis of the data, we determined which qualities had the biggest effect on price and created a Random Forest Machine Learning Model to predict prices of new properties. You can make use of that model to price your property by using the form on the Home page.

On This Page

The map on this page displays an overview of Airbnb rental prices by neighborhood. Based on the color scale, you can see the most expensive neighborhoods in Amsterdam compared to the least expensive.

Our product is not only intended to be used by property owners; we also provide a solution to renters looking to evaluate the price ranges of properties throughout the city. This map allows renters to quickly narrow down their search based on their budget.

Hover through the neighborhoods on the map to find more information including the price range of specific neighborhoods.
